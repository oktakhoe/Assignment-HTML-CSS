Scholastic celebrates 25 years of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Published on Sep 11th 2023

C

an you believe it? It is officially 25 years since the first Harry Potter book hit the shelves in the US. And Scholastic have a huge celebration planned, including free book giveaways, competitions, and a very special Empire State Building tower lighting.

First up, prepare to get a little bit teary at this new sizzle reel put together by Scholastic, rolling back the years…





And here’s everything you need to know about how Scholastic are celebrating. Including a rather illuminating moment in New York…

The Empire State Building will light up in Hogwarts house colours

On 27th September, the world-famous Empire State Building will light up Gryffindor red, Slytherin Green, Ravenclaw blue and Hufflepuff yellow to commemorate this huge milestone in the Harry Potter calendar. If you live in the city that doesn’t sleep, you’ll definitely not want to miss this one.



Scholastic will donate 25,000 copies of Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone to schools

Across the US, kids, teachers, and school librarians will receive free copies of Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone, in collaboration with the Scripps Howard Fund. The national literacy campaign “If You Give a Child a Book...” focuses on providing free books to low-income students. Additionally, all seven Harry Potter books will be gifted to school libraries in underserved communities.

Get ready for the “Welcome to Hogwarts” National Contest

“What class would you take at Hogwarts and why?” – that is the question being posed to young fans with the chance of winning an incredible prize. Competition entrants simply need to answer the question – then three lucky winners and their guests will be whisked off to The Wizarding World of Harry Potter™ at the location of their choice, Universal Studios Hollywood or Universal Orlando Resort. Other prizes include a Harry Potter book box set and a classroom set of Harry Potter books to donate to a school of the winner’s choice, among other magical items. If only Felix Felicis was available to help you win!

Free Harry Potter “Welcome to Hogwarts” Book Kits

The Hogwarts goodies don’t end there. To bring communities together and to encourage kids and families to start their own reading journey, Scholastic this month will be distributing thousands of free Harry Potter “Welcome to Hogwarts” Book Club kits to schools and libraries around the country. Each kit will feature fun activities, discussion guides, stickers and bookmarks.



A new paperback box set

Enjoy the Harry Potter books with refreshed covers, featuring the iconic Harry Potter logo from artist Mary GrandPré – the incredible talent behind the artwork on the original US Harry Potter books.



And many more exciting tomes

There are three more titles to take a look at this year to get in on the excitement, from creative cooking to an encyclopaedic exploration of Harry Potter.
